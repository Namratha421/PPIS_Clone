About PPIS

We at PPIS, utilise our rich experience served in various electrical industries from last two decades, having right knowledge, skill in the field of energy sector. Our idea is to work on innovative technologies, services & solutions in various field including energy transmission & distribution. Team is having knowledge in underground cabling systems, sub-station equipment’s, overhead network with bare conductors, insulated conductors, AB cables & it’s accessories. Work experience with various utilities, industries & strategic customers like railways, metros, defence, wind, solar etc. We provide proper consultation, pre & post installation & maintenance training.

Our Team

Veer Raj Naik


Executive Director

Veer Raj Naik

Veer Raj is an Energy Transmission & Distribution Industry veteran and Co-founder of PPIS. Before founding the company, Veer has worked in senior roles, over a career spanning 2 decades in Marketing Energy Products & Technical Services with some of the industry leaders like Raychem RPG, KEC International, Dubai Electricity and Water Authority (DEWA), TPC FZE (Dubai) and Mahindra Group. During the stints with these companies, he has handled roles like Chief Manager – Exports and Regional Manager (South & North) where he has worked closely with some of top Executives in India and Dubai. He is passionate about meeting clients across continents and solving complex problems for them. He has travelled to and has business exposure to many countries including Dubai, Australia, Germany, Sweden, Denmark, Czech Republic, Austria, New Zealand, South Africa and Nepal during his Corporate career.
